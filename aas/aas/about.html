AAS (Aam Aadmi Seva)

We aim is to provide an open platform to all the citizens of our country on which they can vocalize and localize their work.Irrespective of the sector in which they belong. To make a webapp that will be for all types of people whether they are farmers, businessmen, exporters, importers, teachers, traders, or anyone else. To serve auser-friendly environment so that people can interact with each other and work smoothly and effectively. To serve someadditional features through a web page such as a chat option so that buyers can connect with sellers directly, a platform where teachers can share their study material among students by just sharing a key, and many more.

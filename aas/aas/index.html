About Us

We aim is to provide an open platform to all the citizens of our country on which they can vocalize and localize their work.Irrespective of the sector in which they belong. To make a webapp that will be for all types of people whether they are farmers, businessmen, exporters, importers, teachers, traders, or anyone else.
To serve auser-friendly environment so that people can interact with each other and work smoothly and effectively. To serve someadditional features through a web page such as a chat option so that buyers can connect with sellers directly, a platform where teachers can share their study material among students by just sharing a key, and many more.

Local for Vocal

A tech platform to connect workers with employment!

With this channel, we will provide a platform where small businessmen can showcase their work and from here only big companies can hire them to whether to collaborate with small businesses or to open a new branch with them.
By this, small business will get support and they can raise too. They can find buyers for their products also. On the other hand, big manufacturers who are searching for skilled laborers and labors who are searching for work can also connect with the medium of chat or call system.This way unemployed people will get jobs and companies will get employees according to their vacancies.

EDUCATION FOR EVERONE

A tech platform that provide us the Education!

As seen nowadays people are more into internet but at this point most of the students even teachers are facing obstacles regarding network issue.
AAS provides you a network to connect people around the world. Teachers can upload their study material or post their profile to the EDUCATION ,so that if a student needs or searching for a tutor ,he/she can access from there.
It provides you a secure desktop connection for sharing data.Fast data transmission without much use of data.We know how critical these are when working on low network connection.That’s why our innovative idea for accessing the data just requires copy and paste.We provide you a key and you can access it from there.
